I2C µBackpack for 8x2 character LCD
Few words about my I2C µBackpack for 8x2 character LCDs.
I really like using the very common 16x2 character LCDs with I2C backpacks in my arduino projects, they are extremely simple to connect and use in the program and in my (very subjective) opinion they look nice. However, sometimes there just inst the space or even the need to use a wide display like the 16x2, thankfully there are many sizes of these displays. A nice, smaller version has a size of 8x2 characters. Sadly they are way less favorable by the maker comunity and are barely used so there isnt a dedicated I2C backpack made for them. (mainly)
